This is JSS International School in Dubai. The acronym "JSS" stands for Jagathguru Shiva Shradashram. Its headquarters is in Karnataka, India. It has another school in Dubai (JSS Private School). My school follows the CISCE curriculum in India. It has stood first in UAE in the ICSE and ISC examinations and has consistently proven to be one of the best schools in this curriculum in Dubai and the UAE.
